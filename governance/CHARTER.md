# Governance Charter

## Purpose

Provide durable operating rules for the Colony Cults project so research progress survives tool changes, context loss, and contributor turnover.

## Scope

This governance layer applies to project state, research workflow, session hygiene, and decision recording in the public repository.

## Charter commitments

- The repository is the authoritative memory for project state.
- Evidence and provenance come before synthesis.
- Copyright limits what may be mirrored, not what may be cataloged.
- Uncertainty should be recorded explicitly.
- Small durable commits are preferred to large fragile batches.
- Session continuity should depend on files in the repository, not on any one contributor or chat transcript.
