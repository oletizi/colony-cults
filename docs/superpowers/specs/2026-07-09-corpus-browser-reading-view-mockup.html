Colony Cults · reading-view study

The prospectus,
held as evidence.

La Nouvelle France sold a paradise that did not exist — a "Free Colony" at Port-Breton whose investors were defrauded and whose settlers died. The browser's job is to let a reader hold the propaganda page, its French words, and an English translation at once, inside an archival frame that never lets the source pass for truth.

Three layouts below, same real front page (15 July 1879), differing only in what is the hero — the facsimile, the parallel text, or the object itself. Pick the reading experience; the identity carries across all three.
