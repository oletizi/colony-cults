# Open Research Questions

## Port Breton / Marquis de Rays

- Did the Marquis de Rays ever visit Port Breton or New Ireland?
- How much money did the enterprise actually raise?
- How many settlers sailed on each expedition?
- How many settlers died, and where?
- How many survivors reached Australia?
- What role did Barcelona play in recruitment, financing, and logistics?
- Which Catholic clergy or institutions supported the enterprise, and why?
- How did French, Italian, Spanish, German, and Australian newspapers frame the affair differently?
- What were the names, routes, and outcomes of each ship connected to the colony?
- What legal findings emerged from the Marquis de Rays trial?
- Which families from the expedition later settled in New Italy?
- What claims did the promotional literature make about land, climate, fertility, and resources?
- Which claims can be checked against later accounts from survivors or officials?
